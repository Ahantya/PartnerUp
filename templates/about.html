What is PartnerUp?

PartnerUp is an online database geared towards connecting students, teachers, and community members to prominent companies particularly in the health sector. The database dynamically updates with each user and allows users to search, filter, and view various health companies.

Who can access the program and partner information?

Authorized users within the Career and Technical Education Department, including faculty, staff, and administrators, have access to the program. This ensures that the information is securely managed and used for educational purposes. Students may also view data, however, have no editing or deleting access.

How does the program benefit the students of the Career and Technical Education Department?

The program serves as a valuable resource for students by providing access to real-world opportunities and resources offered by partner organizations. It helps students explore internships, mentorship programs, and other career-related activities aligned with their interests and career goals.

How can we view more data for each partner?

In order to view more data, you can press the "..." button, where more info such as contact phone number and email address cam be viewed. If you are logged in as an admin, it will also give you an option to edit the data.

How many partners can be added?

Currently, you can add as many partners as you want, including through a CSV file.

What inspired PartnerUp?

Last year, one of our founders was interested in finding a health-related internship for a class at school. This was a difficult but overall enlightening process, as one particular problem he encountered was finding companies to reach out to. With PartnerUp, we solve the problem of connecting students to bio companies to ease future students who are either taking the same class just interested in health, with finding an internship.
